In the Cosmology part, the metric signature is inverted to mostly negative $ (n-1 , 1) $, i.e. $ (+ , - , \dots , -) $, therefore a vector $ X_p \in T_p\man $ is:
\begin{itemize}
  \item timelike if $ X_p^2 \equiv g_p(X_p , X_p) > 0 $;
  \item null if $ X_p^2 \equiv g_p(X_p , X_p) = 0 $;
  \item spacelike if $ X_p^2 \equiv g_p(X_p , X_p) < 0 $;
\end{itemize}

Quantities with a subscript `0' are evaluated at the present coordinate time $ t_0 $.
