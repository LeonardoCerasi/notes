Although the Einstein field equations, being second-order non-linear inhomogeneuos PDEs, are highly non-trivial to solve, they admit wave-like solutions, which produce what are known as \emph{gravitational waves} (GWs). To study GWs, three approaches are possible: perturbative solutions, beyond-leading-order corrections and exact solutions.
